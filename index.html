Acute Kidney Disease

"Acute kidney disease (Also known as Acute Kidney Injury) is a sudden onset of kidney damage that leads to the accumulation of waste products and fluids in the body. The causes of acute Kidney (kidney) disease can range from dehydration to infections, medications, and autoimmune disorders. Our Ayurvedic treatment offers a natural and effective approach to manage & cure Acute Kidney Disease without the need for dialysis or transplant. Ayurvedic remedies can help improve kidney function and prevent further kidney damage."

High Creatinine Treatment

"Creatinine is a waste product produced by the muscles and excreted by the kidneys. High levels of creatinine in the blood can indicate kidney dysfunction or disease. Improved kidney function and reduced inflammation can both be achieved with ayurvedic treatment for high creatinine levels. Take charge of your health and lower the chance of further kidney damage by adopting Herbal Option for High Creatinine Treatment"

Proteinuria Treatment

"Proteinuria is a medical condition where there is an excessive amount of protein in the urine. It can be an indication of serious kidney disease, or other underlying health issues. In such case It become important to seek treatment as soon as possible. Ayurvedic treatment offers a natural and effective approach to managing Proteinuria without the need for dialysis or transplant. Ayurvedic remedies and can help reduce proteinuria, and prevent further kidney damage"

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

"Chronic kidney disease (CKD) is a long-term condition where the kidneys gradually lose their function over time. The causes of CKD include diabetes, high blood pressure, genetic factors, and certain medications. There can be different stages for Chronic Disease (CKD Stage 1, CKD Stage 2, CKD Stage 3, CKD Stage 4 or CKD Stage 5) and each stage require to be treated differently. If you've been diagnosed with Chronic Kidney Disease, Ayurvedic treatment offers a natural and effective approach to cure & managing Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) without the need for dialysis or transplant. With Ayurvedic treatment, you can manage your Chronic Kidney Disease and improve your quality of life."

Nephrotic Syndrome Treatment

"Nephrotic syndrome is a condition that occurs when the kidneys leak too much protein into the urine. Symptoms of nephrotic syndrome include swelling in the legs, feet, and ankles, fatigue, and foamy urine. It can be caused by various underlying conditions such as diabetes, infections, or autoimmune diseases. Ayurvedic treatment offers a natural and effective approach to managing nephrotic syndrome without the need for dialysis or transplant. By reducing inflammation and supporting kidney function, ayurvedic remedies can help improve symptoms and prevent further kidney damage."

Polycystic Kidney Disease

"Polycystic kidney disease (PKD) is a genetic disorder characterized by the formation of numerous cysts in the kidneys. These cysts can cause the kidneys to enlarge and lose function over time. Symptoms of PKD include high blood pressure, back or side pain, blood in the urine, and kidney stones. Ayurvedic treatment offers a natural and effective approach to managing PKD without the need for dialysis or transplant. By supporting kidney function and reducing inflammation, ayurvedic remedies can help manage symptoms and improve the quality of life for those with PKD."

Sign and symptoms of kidney failure

Kidney failure is a slow disease and takes time to advance into later stages. Some of the most common signs that a patient can experience are:

  • Edema which is a condition that causes Swollen body parts
  • Dry and itchy skin
  • Pain in the abdomen
  • Change in the color of urine
  • Muscle cramps and twitches
  • Frequent urge to urinate
  • Loss of appetite and abnormal weight loss or weight gain
